🎭 How to Play

👥
Split into two teams. One person is the actor, the rest are guessers.
📱
The actor holds the phone flat on their forehead, screen facing up — so they can't see it but everyone else can.
🎬
The team acts out the word shown on screen without speaking. The actor tries to guess what it is!
✅
Correct guess? Tilt the phone UP (raise your forehead). Or tap the right side of the screen.
⛔
Want to skip? Tilt the phone DOWN (drop your chin). Or tap the left side of the screen.
⏱️
Score as many correct guesses as you can before the timer runs out. Then swap actors!
