## How to Create Your Own Custom Google Maps (Easy!)

### Method 1: Google My Maps (RECOMMENDED - Best for multiple days)
1. Go to: https://www.google.com/maps/d/
2. Click "Create a New Map"
3. Click "Add layer" for each day (Day 1, Day 2, etc.)
4. For each location below, click "Add marker" and paste the address
5. Customize pin colors, add notes, draw routes
6. Save and access on your phone!

### Method 2: Quick Single-Day Maps
1. Open Google Maps on your phone
2. Search for each location below
3. Tap "Save" ‚Üí Create new list (e.g., "Singapore Day 1")
4. Add all locations for that day
5. Access your saved lists anytime!
