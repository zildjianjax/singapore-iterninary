## üìç LOCATION DETAILS

### Sultan Mosque (Masjid Sultan)
- **Address:** 3 Muscat Street, Singapore 198833
- **Hours:** 10 AM - 12 PM, 2 PM - 4 PM (prayer hall)
- **Entry:** FREE
- **Dress Code:** Modest clothing required (robes provided free)
- **What to Do:** Remove shoes, photos outside & respectfully inside
- **Time Needed:** 30 minutes

### Haji Lane
- **Address:** Haji Lane, Kampong Glam (parallel to Arab Street)
- **Hours:** Shops typically 11 AM - 8 PM, open street
- **Entry:** FREE
- **What to Do:** Walk, take photos, browse shops, caf√©s
- **Time Needed:** 30 minutes
- **Tip:** Best light for photos: early afternoon (3-4 PM)

### Tan Teng Niah House
- **Address:** 37 Kerbau Road, Singapore 219163
- **Hours:** Always visible (exterior only, private residence)
- **Entry:** FREE (photos from public road)
- **What to Do:** Take photos from street, don't enter property
- **Time Needed:** 10-15 minutes
- **Tip:** Best without crowds, quick stop

### Merlion Park
- **Address:** 1 Fullerton Road, Singapore 049213
- **Hours:** 24 hours (best during daylight)
- **Entry:** FREE
- **What to Do:** Photos with Merlion, walk waterfront
- **Time Needed:** 30 minutes
- **Tip:** Arrive 2:45 PM for good light, less crowded than morning

### Helix Bridge
- **Address:** Connects Marina Centre to Marina South
- **Hours:** 24 hours
- **Entry:** FREE
- **What to Do:** Walk across, take photos of structure
- **Time Needed:** 20-30 minutes
- **Tip:** Covered walkway, shaded from sun
